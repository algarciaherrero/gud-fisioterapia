Who I am

I know what it feels like
when your body lets you down

I'm Pablo Ruiz, a qualified physiotherapist since 2007 and a crossfit athlete. I have over 15 years of experience working in hospitals, insurance clinics and private practices, and more than 10 years of dedicated training in osteopathy.

I train at high intensity every day, which has taught me something no textbook can: how an injury truly affects your life, your performance and your mind. When you come to my clinic, I don't give you the theory. I understand you.

My goal isn't just to make the pain go away. It's to help you understand what happened and why, so it doesn't happen again.
